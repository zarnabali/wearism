WHY — The Problem We're Solving 
Fashion is broken. Not because we don't have enough clothes—we have too many. The 
average person wears only 20% of their wardrobe regularly, yet still feels like they have 
"nothing to wear." 
• Most clothing sits unused after purchase, wasting money and space 
• Daily outfit decisions create stress and decision fatigue 
• Generic styling advice ignores what you actually own and who you are 
• People keep buying to solve a problem that more shopping can't fix 
• The fashion industry produces 10% of global carbon emissions, and most of it is 
unnecessary 
Despite dozens of wardrobe apps and AI fashion tools, none of them actually understand 
you—your body, your lifestyle, your preferences, and most importantly, what's already 
hanging in your closet. 
That's the gap Wearism fills. 
HOW — Our Approach 
Wearism sits at the center of your fashion life. We connect you, your wardrobe, your friends, 
and the brands you care about through a single intelligent platform. 
Make the most of what you own. Buy smarter when you need to. 
Instead of pushing you to buy more, we help you discover the potential in what you already 
have. When you do need something new, we make sure it actually fits your life and your 
style.

Executive Summary 
Wearism is an AI-driven fashion intelligence platform that revolutionizes how people interact with their 
wardrobes through computer vision, personalized styling, and smart wardrobe management. By 
combining cutting-edge AI technology with practical fashion solutions, Wearism addresses a $1.7 trillion 
annual problem: wardrobe inefficiency and poor outfit decisions. 
The Opportunity at a Glance 
● Market Size: AI in Fashion market valued at $2.92B in 2025, projected to reach $89.41B by 
2035 (40.8% CAGR) 
● Wardrobe App Market: Growing from $1.5B in 2024 to $5.3B by 2033 (15.8% CAGR) 
● Target Users: 500M+ fashion-conscious consumers globally spending 13-17 minutes daily on 
outfit decisions 
● Problem Solved: 25% of clothing goes unworn annually, costing consumers billions in wasted 
purchases 
The Problem: A $1.7 Trillion Fashion Crisis 
Consumer Pain Points 
Daily Decision Fatigue 
● Women spend an average of 17 minutes per day deciding what to wear 
● Men spend 13 minutes per day on outfit selection 
● This translates to 100+ hours annually wasted per person 
Massive Wardrobe Inefficiency 
● 25% of clothing items remain unworn for over a year 
● Average American spends $1,700 annually on apparel 
● 30-40% of wardrobe purchases are regretted within months 
Lack of Personalized Guidance 
● No understanding of what suits individual body types, age, and occasions 
● Generic fashion advice doesn't account for personal attributes 
● Confusion about dress codes leads to inappropriate outfit choices 
Why Existing Solutions Fall Short 
Vectr3D: Focuses solely on 3D virtual try-ons without addressing styling intelligence, user analysis, or 
recommendations. 
Assmbl: Uses generic AI (Google Gemini) that produces unreliable fashion advice, sometimes even for 
non-clothing objects like keyboards and tools. 
Traditional Apps: Offer basic wardrobe cataloging but lack AI-driven attribute detection and 
personalized fashion intelligence. 
The Gap: No platform integrates age detection, height estimation, body analysis, clothing classification, 
and fashion-theoretic styling into one unified, intelligent system. 
The Wearism Solution: AI Fashion Intelligence 
Core Technology Platform 
Wearism employs a three-module AI architecture that analyzes user images to deliver personalized 
fashion insights: 
Module 1: Age Estimation 
● Automated facial detection and age prediction 
● Fine-tuned deep learning models trained on large-scale datasets 
● Accuracy: 85%+ for age range classification 
● Processing time: 6 seconds per image 
Module 2: Height Estimation with 3D Reconstruction 
● Pose detection and body landmark extraction 
● 3D parametric mesh generation (SMPL-based) 
● Scale estimation for accurate height prediction 
● Processing time: 10 seconds per full-body image 
Module 3: Clothing Classification 
● Trained on DeepFashion dataset (50+ garment categories) 
● Advanced segmentation and feature extraction 
● Multi-item detection and attribute recognition 
● Processing time: 5 seconds per image
